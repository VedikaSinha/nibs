school achievements

Inter school debate on Environment Pollution
Won first prize in Inter school debate on Environment Pollution

With the aim of encouraging friendly discourse among the schools, the NYP Inter-Schools Debating Competition, organized by NYP Current Affairs and Debating Club, is an excellent platform for our students to showcase their oratorical and critical thinking skills.

Inter School Football Competition
Won second prize in Inter School Football Competition

With the aim of encouraging friendly discourse among the schools, Interschool Football Tournament was organized by Prakash Vidyalaya for the boys, in which 16 teams participated. Chandler football team played valiantly to reach the finals against the host team and secured the second position in the finals.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second prize in Inter School Science Project Competition

Run by the Analytical Chemistry Trust Fund and the Royal Society of Chemistry, the annual Schools’ Analyst competition allows students aged 16–17 to expand their chemistry knowledge and skills through practical analytical experiments based on relevant societal or industrial problems.
